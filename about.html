About

In this game, you play as a miner who is on a mission to collect valuable resources from mines. The more mines you own, the more resources you can extract, which can be used as currency in the game. As you progress, you can upgrade your mines and unlock new types of resources to collect. The ultimate goal is to become the richest miner in the land!

You will start with a small amount of currency, and you must use it wisely to purchase your first mine. From there, you must manage your resources effectively, balancing the purchase of new mines and upgrades to your existing ones. You will encounter challenges along the way, such as declining resource yields and competition from other miners, but with strategic thinking and quick reflexes, you will overcome these obstacles and become the richest miner in the land!

So what are you waiting for? Put on your miner's hat and start digging for riches!
